Memories and Thoughts

The Project

We wanted to take advantage of the time we had with Dad (John C. Elsey) in order to create somewhat of a historical sketch of his life, as well as gather some of his thoughts and observations.

Different time periods and aspects of his life were set into 16 different segments, with a few different members of the family volunteering to interview Dad for those segments. Other than a few suggested questions or topics, the interviewers were on their own. I think they did a great job. Thank you to each of you!

The interviews were not done sequentially. They occurred from March through May 2024. Because the interviewers weren’t aware of what the others covered, there are obvious areas of overlap in several cases. There was even combining of segments- #4, “Family Activities” covers both those with Dad’s parents as well as those with his children (originally intended to be #13- so it’s absence has nothing to do with superstition). There are also guest appearances from Mom (Mavis L. Elsey) from time to time. And, unfortunately, along the way, the audio for a couple segments was irretrievably lost- but the transcripts survived.

The final segment was added later- an opportunity to collect from Dad some overarching thoughts regarding life and his life.

In the transcripts, the interviewers are identified, along with the date when the interview took place.

Although accuracy was attempted, it was probably not achieved as much as desired in the transcripts. However, the general gist of the conversation is there. But to get the full effect- the laughter, the intonation, the feeling of the responses, etc. - listening to the audio would be best. And it’s good to know that the voices are recorded for years to come.

Enjoy

John R. Elsey
